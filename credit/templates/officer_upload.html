CSV Format

Your CSV file should have the following columns:

  • name - Beneficiary name
  • age - Age (optional)
  • location - Location (optional)
  • income_est - Estimated income (optional)
  • loan_amount - Loan amount (optional)
  • tenure - Loan tenure in months (optional)
  • repayment_status - Repayment status (optional)
  • electricity_bill - Electricity bill (optional)
  • mobile_bill - Mobile bill (optional)
  • other_bills - Other bills (optional)
